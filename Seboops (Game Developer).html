Seboops (aka Stropus) is a solo indie developer active since December 2021, best known for crafting physics-based platformers, action-puzzle games, and reusable asset packs on itch.io. https://seboops.itch.io

Notable Works:

**Roll It!** – A challenging physics platformer in which the player rolls, slams, and dodges through obstacle-filled levels; features timed slams, unlockable skins, and multiple stages. Active development appears to have ceased as of early 2023; no public builds beyond the browser demo have been released. https://seboops.itch.io/roll-it/devlog **Pop the Tick!** – An action-puzzle game centered on “tick” popping with resource management via a “Lemon Meter”; includes a settings menu, quick weapon switching, and shop balance adjustments. Actively updated with quality-of-life improvements. https://seboops.itch.io/pop-the-tick/devlog/980679/settings-menu-shop-nerfs-and-quick-weapon-switching **RollIt Launchpad Light** – A free asset pack providing animated launchpad sprites with transparent backgrounds, licensed for commercial use and suitable for physics-based projects. https://seboops.itch.io/rollit-launchpad-light
